Sandwich Recipe

A simple cheese ham sandwich (image source from Seriouseats.com)

Ingredients:

  • 2x Slices of Bread
  • 1x Slice of Cheese
  • 2x Slices of Ham
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Place the two slices of bread on a plate.
  2. With a butter knife, apply Mayonnaise to the top side of both slices of bread.
  3. Place one slice of ham to both slices of bread.
  4. Place one slice of cheese ontop of sliced ham.
  5. Put the two slices of bread together and you're done!
