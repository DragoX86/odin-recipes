Spaghetti Recipe

A simple spaghetti dish (Recipe Source)

Ingredients:

  • 1x onion
  • 1x bell pepper
  • 29.58 ml of garlic powder
  • 44.37 ml of butter
  • 4.92 ml of salt
  • 4.92 ml of pepper
  • 2x (850.48 g) cans of tomato sauce
  • 453.59g box of spaghetti noodles
  • 453.59-680.38 g of hamburger meat

Steps:

  1. Chop the onion and bell pepper to small pieces.
  2. On medium heat melt the butter and sautee the onion and bell peppers.
  3. Add the hamburger meat and cook until meat is well done.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  5. Salt, pepper and garlic powder can be adjusted to your own tastes.
  6. Cook noodles as directed.
  7. Mix the sauce and noodles if you like, I keep them separated.
